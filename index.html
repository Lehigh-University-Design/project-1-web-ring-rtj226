Bangkok, Thailand After years of traveling, here are some tips for young travelers:
  • Take advantage of public transportation!
  • Try something new! Food, experience, museum, anything
  • Talk to locals, make friends abroad!
  • Give yourself grace, take breaks, you don't always have to go out
  • Be open to getting lost, then find your way back
  • Cash is key. Never assume a country will accept credit cards
